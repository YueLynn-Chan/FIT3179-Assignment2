What are UFOs?

"Unidentified flying objects (UFOs) also known as unidentified anomalous phenomenon (UAP) are any perceived airbonre, submerged or transmedium phenomenon that cannot be immediately identified or explained." - Wikipedia (17/10/2024)

While there has been many unusual sightings in the sky throughout history, UFOs became culturally prominent after WW2, which escalated during the Space Age and the Cold War.

This data visualization aims to show 80,000 observations of these UFOs across the century, including the shape observed, duration spotted, the descriptions of the UFOs and such.
